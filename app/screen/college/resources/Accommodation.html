Accommodation

"DISCOVER THE BEST STUDENT LIVING EXPERIENCE"

We offer reasonably priced and comfortable hostel accommodation to our full-time students. Our hostel is closely located to the Weld Quay area where there are plenty of eateries and hawker food. Students may travel to the campus by foot, by bus, or by SENTRAL's van service.

SENTRAL's hostel is committed to supporting you with the best living experience by providing:

  • Wi-Fi access
  • Conducive living and learning spaces
  • Safe and high-security environment (inclusive of a CCTV system and smart card access)
  • 24 hours support via our live-in housemaster
  • Separate housing for male and female residents
  • Various types of room selection to suit your needs: single rooms, twin sharing rooms, triple sharing rooms, and quad sharing rooms
  • Pantry section
  • Washing area equipped with a washing machine, clothes dryer and water dispenser
